Our Approach

Investment Philosophy

We follow a fundamental, value-oriented investment strategy and seek to outperform the market. We make concentrated investments in companies that are out of favor with institutional investors. We believe our academic focus detaches us from short-term fluctuations and provides us with a unique perspective and the ability to take advantage of market overreactions.

Importantly, we do not make macroeconomic predictions nor do we invest in companies highly correlated to the macroeconomic environment. Instead, we perform bottom-up analysis and look for company-specific investment opportunities that are attractive in all macro situations.

Investment Process

Our investment process is a rigorous two-stage evaluation process. First, industry groups pitch investment ideas to the Partners - whom collectively decide on inclusion into the portfolio. Then if included, position sizing is based on conviction and risk considerations.


Our Focus

Investment Universe

Our investment universe includes the equity securities of small and mid cap companies, which tend to have less sell-side coverage and lower institutional ownership, resulting in greater mispricing opportunities. Our focus is on U.S. equities, but we also invest opportunistically internationally. Our investment time horizon is 1-3 years.

Investment Sourcing

We source investment ideas from our five industry groups: Consumer Products, Industrials & Materials, Healthcare, Energy, and Technology, Media & Telecommunications (TMT).
