Our Approach

Investment Objective

Our primary investment objective is to learn about investing. Thus, we believe maintaining a concentrated portfolio enables us to maximize learning and have a deep understanding of all our holdings.

Investment Philosophy

Our investment philosophy is fundamental value-oriented investing. We seek to make investments in companies with large margins of safety.

Investment Process

Our investment process is a rigorous two-stage discussion of the investment idea. We strive to take advantage of our academic background to engage in a research-intensive approach to investing.


Our Focus

Investment Universe

Our investment universe includes equity securitires of small and mid cap companies. Our focus is on US equities, but we also invest opportunistically internationally. Our investment time horizon is 2-3 years.

Investment Sourcing

Our fund sources investment ideas from six Industry Groups led by our Partners: Consumer Products, Global Macro, Industrials & Materials, Healthcare, Energy, and Technology, Media & Telecommunications (TMT).
