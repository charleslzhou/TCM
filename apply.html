We are a rapidly growing organization, and we look for intellectually curious and motivated students who are passionate about investing and want to contribute to the development of TCM. Our available positions are listed below.

Junior Analyst – assists with research, industry analysis, and developing stock pitches under the guidance of a partner, and under the supervision of senior analysts.

Senior Analyst – contributes to investment sourcing within the industry and takes lead on research, analysis, and due diligence requested by the partner, while closely working with the junior analysts.

Partner – directs research into prospective investments, leads industry-specific stock pitches to the club, and has decision-making power over investment decisions.
