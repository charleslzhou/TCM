Our Mission

Our mission is to provide Princeton undergraduate students with practical investing experience through the pursuit of market-beating returns.


Connecting Ideas

We seek to capitalize on the confluence of disciplines, spectrum of investing experience, and breadth of academic specialties, which lead to a diversity of thought and a robust discussion of investment ideas. Our focus on conducting independent, proprietary research takes advantage of Princeton’s background as a liberal arts research institution and encourages an exceptional environment for learning and discovery.


Closing Gaps

We strive to bridge the gap between financial theory and practice in Princeton’s undergraduate investing world. Our belief is that by aiming to outperform markets with appreciable capital, we can offer an unparalleled, hands-on investing experience and further develop students into savvy investors.



Our History

Founded in July 2013, Tiger Capital Management LLP is currently the only student-run, privately managed investment fund at Princeton. Our five founding members envisioned creating a venue for openly discuss investing ideas and being able to accurately measure the level of conviction in investment theses. In order to provide such a forum for exchanging ideas, TCM was formed and continues to drive its mission of enabling practical investing education by pursuing superior risk-adjusted returns.
