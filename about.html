Our Mission

Our mission is to provide Princeton undergraduate students with practical investing experience through the pursuit of market-beating returns.



Our Focus

Investment Universe

We are a long only equity fund investing in primarily small and mid cap companies, with a 2-3 year investment horizon. Our focus is on US equities, but also invest opportunistically internationally. Our fund sources ideas from five vertical teams: Consumer, Technology, Industrials, Global, Healthcare, and Energy.



Our Approach

Investment Philosophy

Our primary investment objective is to learn about investing. In pursuit of this objective, we maintain a concentrated portfolio of small and mid cap companies, which enables us to gain an edge and have a deep understanding of all our holdings.


Investment Process

We take advantage of our academic background and our distance from Wall Street noise to take a research-intensive approach to investing. Our fund sources its ideas from six internal research groups, Global, Industrials, Consumer, Energy, Healthcare, and Technology, led by the fundâ€™s partners. Each investment idea undergoes a rigorous two-stage process that includes an initial pitch and investment note, followed by a discussion of entry/exit strategy.



Our History

Founded in July 2013, Tiger Capital Management LLP is currently the only student-run, privately managed investment fund at Princeton. Our five founding members envisioned creating a venue for openly discuss investing ideas and being able to accurately measure the level of conviction in investment theses. In order to provide such a forum for exchanging ideas, TCM was formed and continues to drive its mission of enabling practical investing education by pursuing superior risk-adjusted returns.


Founding Principles

Three Founding Principles drive our philosophy:

  • Intellectual Curiosity, that are beliefs are constantly challenge.
  • Partnership, that our decision making process is flat and meritocratic.
  • Engagement, that our members are actively involved and constantly voice new ideas.
